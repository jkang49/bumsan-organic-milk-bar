About Us

Bumsan Organic Milk Bar serves premium 100% organic soft serve ice cream from the leading organic dairy producer in Korea, Bumsan Farm Co., who maintains sustainable farming practices and produces 100% organic USDA certified, non-GMO, antibiotic-free dairy products. Our goal is to bring this unique treat that has gained massive popularity in Korea to the U.S. market.
